About Medical Diagnosis AI

Advanced machine learning for diabetes risk assessment

Project Overview

This medical diagnosis classifier uses advanced machine learning algorithms to predict diabetes risk based on patient health metrics and lifestyle factors.

Objectives
  • Early diabetes risk detection
  • Personalized health assessments
  • Educational health awareness
  • Accessible AI-powered healthcare
Key Features
  • Real-time risk assessment
  • Multiple ML model support
  • Comprehensive health metrics
  • Interactive web interface

Technical Implementation

Machine Learning
  • Algorithm: Logistic Regression
  • Alternative: Random Forest
  • Validation: Cross-validation
  • Optimization: Grid Search
Data Processing
  • Features: 8 health metrics
  • Scaling: StandardScaler
  • Dataset: 1000+ patients
  • Split: 80/20 train/test
Web Technology
  • Backend: Flask (Python)
  • Frontend: Bootstrap 5
  • API: RESTful endpoints
  • UI: Responsive design

Health Metrics Analyzed

Age

Diabetes risk increases with age, particularly after 45 years.

BMI (Body Mass Index)

Higher BMI correlates with increased diabetes risk due to insulin resistance.

Glucose Level

Fasting glucose levels indicate current blood sugar control status.

Blood Pressure

Hypertension often accompanies diabetes and metabolic syndrome.

Gender

Gender influences diabetes risk patterns and hormonal factors.

Family History

Genetic predisposition significantly affects diabetes risk.

Physical Activity

Regular exercise improves insulin sensitivity and glucose metabolism.

Smoking Status

Smoking increases diabetes risk and complicates blood sugar control.
